TOKENOMICS

Half the Tokens for Deflationary Impact through Buybacks and Burns



HARAMBE token implements a unique tokenomic strategy that embraces deflationary principles. By allocating half of the HARAMBE tokens for this purpose, the protocol engages in regular buybacks and burns, creating scarcity and sustainable value for its holders. This innovative approach ensures a dynamic ecosystem for HARAMBE, driving its potential as a revolutionary token in the market.
